About

Studio AKASAKA is an independent jewellery studio based in Berlin. The work begins with a simple act: touching the material.

Metal is heated, hammered, and allowed to move. Through this process, forces that are usually invisible—pressure, heat, and resistance—leave traces on the surface. The ERDE series takes its name from the German word for “earth.” Rather than imitating the appearance of soil or landscape, the work reflects the forces that shape the earth itself. The marks on the metal are records of transformation.

Growing up in Japan, where earthquakes and volcanic activity are part of everyday life, the power of the land is something I have always felt directly. This awareness continues to shape the way I work with material.

Metal carries its own time. There is the geological time contained within the material, the moment of making, and the time of the person who eventually wears it.
Jewellery becomes a point where these different times meet.

Studio AKASAKA is also a place. Objects are made here slowly, one by one, in dialogue with the material.

The studio opens once a month in Berlin.

Materials

  • 18k Gold
  • Selected natural stones
