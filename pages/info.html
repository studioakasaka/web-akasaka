About

Studio AKASAKA is a Berlin-based jewelry practice founded by Takehiro Akasaka.

Each piece is handcrafted in Berlin, primarily working with silver and gold. The work explores transformation through hammering and heat — revealing the inherent character of metal rather than imposing decorative form.

The practice reflects on subtle, often invisible forces that shape matter and human experience.

Works are produced in small quantities or as one-of-a-kind pieces.

Materials

  • Sterling Silver (925)
  • 18k Gold
  • Selected natural stones

Natural variations and traces of the making process are part of the work.
